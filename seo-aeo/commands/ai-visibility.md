# AI Visibility Check

Test how visible your brand is across AI answer engines.

## Usage
```
/seo-aeo:ai-visibility <brand-name> [--queries "query1,query2,query3"]
```

## What it does
1. Tests brand queries across AI platforms (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI)
2. Checks if your brand is mentioned, cited, or linked
3. Analyzes mention sentiment and entity accuracy
4. Calculates AI Share of Voice vs competitors
5. Identifies which content gets cited most
6. Stores results in Supabase for trend tracking

Uses the `ai-citation-tracker` skill.
