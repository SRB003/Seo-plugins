# Share of Model

Measure your brand's Share of Model across AI answer engines.

## Usage
```
/seo-geo:som <brand-name> [--queries "q1,q2,q3"] [--competitors "c1,c2"]
```

## What it does
1. Tests brand queries across AI platforms
2. Calculates Share of Model (your mentions / total brand mentions)
3. Breaks down by platform and topic
4. Compares against competitors
5. Tracks trend over time in Supabase

Uses the `brand-authority-builder` and AEO `ai-citation-tracker` skills.
